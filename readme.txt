Books4All

Books4All is a web platform designed to facilitate the buying and selling of used books. Our goal is to promote environmental sustainability by encouraging the reuse of books, thereby increasing their availability and reducing waste.

Features
User Authentication: Sign up and log in to your account.(database not implemented yet)
Buy Books: Browse available books for sale.

Usage
Sign Up / Sign In: Create an account or log in to your existing account.
Browse Books: Explore the available books listed for sale.
Sell Books: List your used books by providing the necessary details.
Purchase Books: Select and confirm the purchase of books you wish to buy.
